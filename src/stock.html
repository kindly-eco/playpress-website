Stocking Playpress

Stocking our affordable and eco-friendly playsets could not be easier!

Our catalogue

We will send you an digital catalogue and an order form that typically enables us to fulfil your order in a couple of days or earlier.

Free samples

If you would like to receive a sample of our playsets before you order, we will try send it out on the same day of your request.

Stocking Playpress is great because:

  • Our toys take up a small amount of shelf space
  • High visual impact in-store
  • Being flat-pack-fun they post extremely well
  • Eco-friendly, plastic-free credentials
  • No minimum order and free delivery on all orders
